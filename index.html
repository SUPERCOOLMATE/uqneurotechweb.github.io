What We Do

At the Neuro Technology Club, we delve into the fascinating world of neurotechnology, exploring the intersections of neuroscience, technology, and artificial intelligence. Our activities include hands-on workshops, stimulating guest lectures, innovative hackathons, and collaborative research projects. We aim to empower students, spark their curiosity, and equip them with the skills and knowledge to become future leaders in this exciting field. Through our endeavours, we aspire to contribute to the neurotechnology community, both within the University of Queensland and beyond.

Who We Are

We are a dynamic team of three founders, all engineering students with a shared passion for neurotechnology, backed by a group of dedicated executive members. Each of us brings a unique perspective and set of skills, united by our commitment to explore and innovate in the field of neurotechnology. Our members represent a range of disciplines, including biomedical science, computer science and more. We all share the same drive: to engage with cutting-edge technology and leverage it to understand the human brain better.

Our Work

Our work is rooted in the belief that neurotechnology holds enormous potential for societal impact. Through our it, we strive to foster an environment of learning, innovation, and collaboration that empowers every member.
